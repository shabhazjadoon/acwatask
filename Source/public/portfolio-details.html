Barka 1 IWPP

Sultanate of Oman, Barka

Overview

Barka 1 is the first IWPP developed as part of the privatization program of the Sultanate of Oman. It was also the first dual purpose power generation and desalination plant 100% owned by international and local investors.

The plant began commercial operations in June 2003, and a majority stake was acquired by ACWA Power in August 2010. This facility contributes 6% of the electricity and 24% of the desalinated water in Oman.

Key facts

Location 60 km north of Muscat, Oman
OFFTAKER Oman Power and Water Procurement Company
FUEL Natural Gas
POWER 427 MW
CONFIGURATION Gas & Steam Turbines - CCGT
DESALINATED WATER 91,000 m3/day
DESALINATION PROCESS Multi-Stage Flash (MSF)
PROJECT COST USD 415 Mn
